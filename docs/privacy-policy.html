Privacy Policy — SG EV Charging

Last updated: 21 June 2026

SG EV Charging ("the app") is developed by Tertiary Infotech Academy Pte. Ltd. ("we", "us"). This policy explains what data the app uses and how.

Information we use

  • Approximate and precise location: when you tap the location button and grant permission, the app reads your device location on the device to find and rank nearby EV charging points. Your location is used only while you use this feature.
  • Search input: the postal code or place name you type is sent to the device's geocoding service to resolve a map coordinate.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not collect, store, or transmit your location to our servers.
  • We do not require an account, and we do not track you.
  • We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.
  • The app contains no advertising.

Third-party services

The app uses:

  • Google Maps Platform to display the map. Use is governed by the Google Privacy Policy.
  • LTA DataMall (Singapore Land Transport Authority) to retrieve public EV charging point availability data. No personal data is sent to LTA.

Children

The app is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page.
